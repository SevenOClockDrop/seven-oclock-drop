Privacy Policy for SevenO'Clock DropLast Updated: August 27, 2025Thank you for using SevenO'Clock Drop! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application. Our goal is to be as transparent as possible while collecting the minimum amount of data necessary to make the app work.1. Information We CollectWhen you use our app, we collect the following information:• Your Pi Network User ID (UID): This is a unique, anonymous identifier provided by the Pi Network when you log in. We use this to create and manage your account in our system, specifically to track your entries for the daily draw. We do not receive or store your Pi username.2. How We Use Your InformationWe use the information we collect for the following purposes:• To Operate the App: Your UID is essential for us to save your entry count and distinguish your account from other users.• To Manage Entries: We use your UID to associate purchased entries and referral bonuses with your specific account.3. Information We Do Not CollectWe do not collect, store, or have access to any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or your Pi wallet's private keys. All payments are handled securely through the Pi SDK, and we are only notified of a payment's success or failure, not your personal financial details.4. Data Storage and SecurityYour anonymous UID and your current entry count are stored securely in our database, which is hosted by Supabase. We take reasonable measures to protect this information from unauthorized access.5. Changes to This Privacy PolicyWe may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.6. Contact UsIf you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the project creator, @cherylochoa, through the Pi ecosystem.
